Evaluated & excluded

These were checked against our test — own-URL vs. forced infrastructure, not paid vs. free — and left off the list. Re-add any only if we decide to run submissions on their platform.

Submittable — verified Jun 26, 2026: no organizer option to list a call that links artists to our own form. Its Discover directory requires being a paying Submittable org and routes every applicant through a Submittable-hosted form and account; its “External Submissions” tool is just a submitter-side tracker.
CaFÉ, ZAPPlication, CuratorSpace, Zealous, ShowSubmit, EntryThingy (paid hosted form) — excluded for the same reason: their only path makes artists submit inside their system, building their database.
